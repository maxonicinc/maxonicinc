## About Us

Maxonic Inc. is a certified minority-owned staffing and payroll services firm founded in 2002 and headquartered in Campbell, California.

We provide consulting, permanent staffing, and workforce solutions to organizations across North America, Europe, and India. Our experienced recruiting teams leverage an internal database of over one million candidates along with industry-leading sourcing technologies to deliver exceptional talent.
